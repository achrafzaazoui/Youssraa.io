Section I

💖 Love & Pure Heart

💖

A Heart of Light

اللهم اجعل قلب يسرى مطمئنًا بذكرك

May her heart always find rest in faith, love, and the quiet beauty of knowing she is deeply cherished by the universe itself.

🌹

Pure Love

اللهم أحط يسرى بمن يحبها لوجهك

She carries a love so pure it leaves traces of light wherever she walks. The stars themselves bow to the warmth within her soul.

🕊️

Divine Warmth

اللهم اغمر قلبها بمحبتك وحنانك

Allah's love for her is infinite — a warm ocean without shores, a light that never dims, a mercy that wraps around her every breath.

"Every star in the sky carries a prayer for Youssra. The moon keeps vigil over her dreams."

✨ Success & Dreams

✨

Boundless Success

Dreams Beyond Stars

🏆

Destined for Greatness

The universe is conspiring in her favor. Every step she takes is watched over, every effort multiplied, every sacrifice honored by the Divine.

Section VII

🤲 Spiritual Duas

🤲

Dua of Light

اللهم اجعل ليسرى نورًا في قلبها ونورًا في بصرها

May light fill every corner of her being — her mind, her heart, her sight, her path, her past, and every tomorrow yet to come.

🌿

Dua of Ease

اللهم يسِّر لها الأمور وأصلح لها شأنها كله

Where there is difficulty, may Allah open doors. Where there are walls, may He grant wings. Her ease comes from the Most Merciful.

🌺

Dua of the Heart

اللهم اجعل قلب يسرى مليئًا بالأمل والإيمان

A heart full of hope is a heart that cannot be defeated. May her iman be her anchor, her light, and her greatest companion.

🌙

Dua of the Night

اللهم آنسها في وحدتها وأرحها في تعبها

In the quiet of night when the world sleeps, may she feel seen, held, and loved by the Creator who never sleeps.

⭐

Dua of the Morning

اللهم اجعل كل صباح ليسرى بداية جديدة وجميلة

Every dawn is a gift wrapped just for her. A new beginning. A fresh mercy. A chance to rise even higher than yesterday.

💎

Dua of the Future

اللهم اجعل نهايتها خيرًا من بدايتها

Her best chapters are still unwritten. The most beautiful version of her story is the one Allah is authoring right now, with love.

"يُسرى"
